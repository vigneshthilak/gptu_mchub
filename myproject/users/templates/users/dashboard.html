Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Electrical Engineering is a technical discipline concerned with the study, design and application of equipment, devices and systems which use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.

Trained electrical engineers design electric circuits and equipment. They work on large power plants as well in small hardware companies which includes designing, manufacturing and operating power plants, industrial machinery, electrical motors, computer chips and ignition systems for automobiles, aircrafts, space crafts and all kinds of engines.

Department of Computer Engineering

The Department of Computer Engineering was started in the year 2013 and offers 3 years diploma course in computer Engineering to provide an excellent knowledge to the students in computer Engineering in order to meet the challenges of the future , including employment skill in high technology workforce and the ability to pursue higher studies. Train them as future technocrats with basic knowledge in both theory and practice in Computer Engineering, including current emerging technologies and experimental learning.

Our Mission

The mission of the department is to
Impart technical education and knowledge through quality training, hands on experience in the field of computer engineering.
Effectively apply this education to solve real-world problems.
Groom students with social responsibility needed for career advancement and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

The vision of Computer Engineering department is to achieve and evolve as a centre of academic excellence in the frontier areas of computer engineering that responds swiftly to the challenges of the 21st century for the benefit of society.

Lab Facilities

The Computer Engineering Department has well equipped systems with high configuration in all the labs. This department has two fully equipped labs, Networking and Hardware Lab and Software and Web Development Lab.
In Networking and Hardware Lab, all the systems are well connected and virus free. This lab will be exclusively used for the purpose of Network and Hardware Training. All the systems are connected in a secure LAN with Internet facility.
In Software and Web Development Lab, the systems installed with high-end software for Software Development and students are getting trained with good programming skill.

Value added courses offered by the Department

This Department offers value added courses for the benefit of students to enhance their skill in application development in addition to the regular academic courses. These value courses are offered by the department faculties along with the industry peoples. Most of the programs are 10 to 15 days training programs and the following value added courses programs are

  1. Advanced Python Training
  2. Basic C and C++ Programming Skill
  3. Hardware Training
  4. Network Troubleshooting

Technical Concepts based on

  1. Data Structure and Algorithm
  2. Operating System
  3. Programming Languages(C,Java,.NET,Python)
  4. Computer Networking
  5. Computer Hardware and Network Servicing
  6. Database
  7. Web Programming
  8. Cloud computing
  9. Internet of Things
  10. Augmented Reality(AR) & Virtual Reality(VR)
  11. Digital Design
  12. Computer Architecture

Department of Mechatronics Engineering

Diploma in Mechatronics Engineering is a diploma certificate program having a duration of three years.It can be pursued by students who have passed 10th or 12th in the science stream. Mechatronics Engineering which is a newer branch of engineering in comparison to mechanical or electronics engineering has its origins from these branches only.

The curriculum of mechatronics engineering comprises of a lot of elements from Mechanical, Electronics, Computer Science and Control Engineering.A mechatronics engineer uses principles of electronics, computer science, mechanical to develop systems, machine and solutions that can be applied to improve productivity and efficiency of process/production. Systems developed by them have wide applicability in industries.

{% elif mentor_department == 'dmt' %}

Department of Digital Manufacturing Technologies

Under the agreement, Tata Electronics Private Limited (TEPL), DoTE will develop a diploma course on “Digital Manufacturing Technologies - Earn while Learn model”, an industry-ready curriculum, with the required approval from AICTE and offer the same to students through polytechnics in the state.

The programme outline will be based on three months of classroom sessions and nine months on the job at the TEPL plant. TEPL will provide support for faculty training to upgrade their knowledge of digital and smart manufacturing.

DoTE will also develop or modify the existing diploma courses considering the skill development course at TEPL for the 3rd year of the diploma programme.

The modalities for modification of the curriculum incorporating one-year training at TEPL will jointly be discussed and finalised by DoTE and TEPL for implementation with effect from the academic year 2024-25.

As per reports, Guidance Tamil Nadu also facilitated an MoU between DoTE and Maxbyte.

Maxbyte will set up four centres of excellence for Industry 4.0 which could also be used by the students and faculty of Government College of Engineering, Tirunelveli, Government College of Engineering, Salem, Government College of Engineering, Tiruchy, and Thanthai Periyar Government Institute of Technology, Vellore.
